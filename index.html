The 6-Week Curriculum

Real skills. No prior coding required.

Designed by a production AI Platform Architect — the same concepts powering enterprise AI systems, made accessible for any high school classroom.

WEEK 1
What Is an AI Agent?

What makes AI "agentic" vs just smart. Real-world agents in finance, healthcare, and education. Introduction to the Think → Act → Observe loop.

✦ ActivityFind 3 AI agents in your daily life and map their full reasoning loop on paper.
WEEK 2
The Brain Behind the Agent

How Large Language Models power agent reasoning. The art of prompting — giving instructions an AI can actually execute. Chain-of-thought thinking.

✦ ActivityPrompt engineering challenge: get an AI to write code, summarize a paper, and plan a trip — in one session.
WEEK 3
Tools & Memory

Agents become powerful with tools — web search, calculators, databases. Short-term vs long-term memory. Introduction to RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation).

✦ ActivityBuild a simple Q&A bot with memory using a free no-code platform.
WEEK 4
Multi-Agent Systems

When one agent isn't enough. Orchestrator and worker agents. How enterprise AI teams are structured in the real world — and why it matters.

✦ ActivityDesign a multi-agent system for a school project assistant — on paper, as a team.
WEEK 5
Build Your First Agent

Hands-on project week. Students build, test, and demo their own working AI agent using free no-code tools. Debugging and iteration in real time.

✦ ActivityBuild and demo a research agent that answers any question about a topic you choose.
WEEK 6
AI Safety & the Future

What goes wrong with AI agents? Hallucination, bias, privacy, and the responsibilities of builders. AI career paths and what skills matter most.

✦ ActivityEthics debate: should an AI agent be allowed to send emails on your behalf without asking?
