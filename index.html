What You'll Build

In Week 5, you build one of these.

No coding required. Using free, browser-based tools. Each one is a real, working AI agent you can show to anyone.

🔍
Research Agent

Give it a topic. It researches, summarizes findings, and generates 3 follow-up questions worth exploring. Useful for papers, projects, and curiosity.

Tools: Claude.ai · Zapier AI
📚
Study Buddy Agent

Tell it your subject and weak areas. It generates a personalized study plan with specific topics, time blocks, and practice questions tailored to you.

Tools: Claude.ai · ChatGPT
✍️
Creative Agent

Give it a genre, theme, and length. It writes a short story, then switches roles to critique its own writing. Self-evaluating AI in action.

Tools: Claude.ai · Make.com
