Why this matters

Most students can say “I used AI.” Very few can say “I built an AI agent for my community — and I can explain what could go wrong, who's accountable, and how I protected people's data.” That second sentence is the differentiator on a college application. Keep your build, your policy, and a short demo together as one entry.
